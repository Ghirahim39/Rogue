Defensive Buffs

Buffs that share a category with one another do not stack.
Your best defensive buffs are: Flask of the Titans, Elixir of Fortitude, Spirit of Zanza, and Medivh's Merlot.
If you are not using Sweet Mountain Berry / Grilled Squid or Power Mushroom for the offensive buff, your best food buff is Hardened Mushroom / Dirge's Kickin' Chimaerok Chops.

Defensive buffs that are categorised as potions do stack, but share the same 2-minute cooldown.

Weapon Buffs

Weapon buffs are situational as some enemies are immune to nature damage. In most cases your best option is Deadly Poison V on Main Hand and Corrosive Poison II on Off Hand.
Instant Poison VI is a good choice against trash mobs, as they often die too quickly for Deadly Poison V or Corrosive Poison II to be effective.
