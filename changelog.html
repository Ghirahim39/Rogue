Consumables Enchants Changelog

Changelog

If you notice something missing or something wrong anywhere on this website, please send an in-game mail to Medli, or message "Suzuka / Medli" on the Turtle WoW Discord server.

V1.0.8 (12 March 2025)

  • • All consumables and enchants now display approximate prices.
  • • Prices are based on the monthly average from WoW Auctions.
  • • Enchant prices are calculated using the total cost of required materials; presumed tip not included.
  • • When multiple consumables provide the same buff (e.g., "Sweet Mountain Berry" and "Grilled Squid"), only the cheapest option is listed.
  • • For quest rewards requiring items that can be purchased from the Auction House (e.g., "Spirit of Zanza", which requires Zandalar Honor Tokens obtained via Bijous), prices reflect the cost of purchasing those components.
  • • Poisons and "Flash Powder" display their vendor prices instead of Auction House prices.
  • • The last time prices were updated will be displayed at the top of the changelog page.

V1.0.0 (11 March 2025)

Features:

  • • Lists nearly all relevant raid consumables for a DPS Rogue.
  • • Includes key details such as which buffs stack, the best buffs in each category, and which persist through death.
  • • Also includes a list of all BIS enchants and gear augmentations, where to obtain them, and some cheaper alternatives.
  • • Consumables and enchants link directly to the Turtle WoW database, making it easy to look up how to obtain or craft them.
